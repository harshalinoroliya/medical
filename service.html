Our Services

At our Medical Shop, we offer a wide range of healthcare services to meet your needs. Our team of experienced professionals is here to provide you with the highest level of care and support.

Prescription Filling

We offer quick and accurate prescription filling services. Bring in your prescription, and our pharmacists will ensure you get the correct medication in a timely manner.

Consultation Services

Our pharmacists are available for consultations to discuss your medications, health concerns, and wellness goals. Schedule an appointment today to get personalized advice.

Medication Management

We provide medication management services to help you stay on track with your treatment plan. This includes organizing your medications, setting up reminders, and more.

Health Screenings

Regular health screenings are essential for early detection and prevention of illnesses. We offer blood pressure checks, cholesterol screenings, diabetes screenings, and more.

Vaccinations

Stay up-to-date with your immunizations. We provide vaccinations for flu, shingles, pneumonia, and other common diseases. Walk-ins are welcome, or you can schedule an appointment.

Over-the-Counter Medication

We carry a wide range of over-the-counter medications for common ailments. Our knowledgeable staff can help you choose the right product for your needs.

Medical Supplies

We offer a variety of medical supplies, including first aid kits, wound care products, and mobility aids. Visit our shop to see our full selection.
