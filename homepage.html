Capsules

In medicine, a sac of tissue and blood vessels that surrounds an organ, joint, or tumor. A capsule is also a form used for medicine that is taken by mouth.

Syrup

Syrup is a medicine in the form of a thick liquid containing a sugar solution. The cough medicine is supplied as a thick, sweet syrup. The thick syrup is sweetened with a solution of sugar.

Tablets

A tablet (also known as a pill) is a pharmaceutical oral dosage form (oral solid dosage, or OSD) or solid unit dosage form.
